A comprehensive collection of industry-focused analytics projects developed using Python and Machine Learning. This repository covers the full spectrum of financial intelligence—from traditional **Risk Analytics** to cutting-edge **Ethical AI** and **Explainable AI (XAI)**.

---

## 🚀 Key Learning Modules

### 🏦 1. Core Financial Risk
Advanced labs focused on the pillars of banking and investment security.
* **Credit Risk:** Predictive modeling for loan default behavior.
* **Market Risk:** Analyzing volatility and financial trend forecasting.
* **Fraud Detection:** Leveraging **H2O AutoML** for high-precision anomaly detection.

### ⚖️ 2. Ethical AI & Fairness
Critical labs for modern FinTech, ensuring AI models are unbiased and equitable.
* **Bias Detection:** Auditing loan approval models for disparate impact.
* **Fairness Metrics:** Measuring and mitigating algorithmic bias using Google Colab.
* **Transparency Auditing:** Ensuring AI systems comply with financial regulations.

### 🔍 3. Explainable AI (XAI)
Demystifying complex models to provide "human-readable" financial insights.
* **SHAP & LIME:** Implementing local and global interpretability to explain why a model approved or denied a financial application.
